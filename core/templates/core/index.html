About

Where AI meets
real impact

I'm an AI engineer focused on transforming advanced AI concepts into practical, real-world solutions. My work centers on building intelligent applications, integrating AI systems, and developing tools that solve meaningful problems. My experience includes NLP, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and AI-powered applications. I focus on designing systems that not only leverage machine learning but also deliver tangible value to users. Among my projects are DIAVISION, an AI-powered healthcare platform for medical diagnostics, and a satellite imagery analysis system developed to assess building damage in Gaza's Al-Shati Refugee Camp, supporting humanitarian and data-driven decision-making. I believe technology creates its greatest impact when it is used to address real challenges and support communities that need it most.

My most meaningful project is DIAVISION — an AI-powered healthcare system built to assist in medical diagnostics. I'm also developing a satellite imagery analysis system to detect and assess building damage in Gaza's Al-Shati Refugee Camp, combining humanitarian purpose with cutting-edge computer vision.

I believe the most valuable AI is the kind that serves communities that need it most.
